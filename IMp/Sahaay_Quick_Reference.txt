                    SAHAAY: QUICK REFERENCE CARD
                    From Hackathon Idea to Real Product
================================================================================

PROBLEM (WHO STRUGGLES)
• 150M+ elderly in India managing medications alone
• 50M+ caregivers spending 10+ hours/week reminding relatives
• 5M+ adults with ADHD paralyzed by complex task apps
• Busy families juggling multiple responsibilities

THE INSIGHT
"People who need help most fail at reminder apps because apps require them 
to organize themselves. Sahaay flips this: the AI does the organizing."

CORE SOLUTION: SAHAAY
An AI companion that:
  1. Learns your routine through 5-min WhatsApp conversation (zero forms)
  2. Notices deviations from your baseline (drift detection)
  3. Alerts you proactively ("You usually take medicine at 8 AM, it's 11 AM...")
  4. Tells caregivers only when needed (privacy-first)
  5. Works on WhatsApp + voice (no app install)

KEY DIFFERENTIATOR: DRIFT DETECTION
→ Not just "Reminder at 8 AM"
→ Instead: "You usually confirm by 9 AM. It's 11 AM and you haven't. OK?"
→ This is what competitors can't easily copy.

================================================================================
                        TECHNICAL STACK (MVP)
================================================================================

API:              Claude (claude-opus-4-6) for conversation + routing
Messaging:        Twilio (WhatsApp + SMS)
Backend:          FastAPI (Python web framework)
Database:         PostgreSQL (routines, logs) + Redis (session state)
Scheduling:       Celery + Redis (reminders, drift detection)
Hosting:          AWS EC2 t3.micro (~$8/month) or local dev

TOTAL COST (Month 1): ~₹50–500 (just APIs + local dev)
TOTAL COST (Month 3, 100 users): ~₹3,000–4,000/month
